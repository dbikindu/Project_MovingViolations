Report: Moving Violation & Traffic Cameras

Our project Datasets are derived from 'opendatadc.org'. The Datasets include "Moving Violations" issue in september 2018 and 'Traffic Camera' location. We used 'leaflet' and 'geojson' to visualize the traffic violations in District of Columbia (D.C.). We also used 'chart.js' which is a library we did not cover in class for graph showing the correlation between the violation locations and the fines.

D.C. has a complicated relationship with speed cameras. Research shows that speed cameras are an important tool to reduce crashes and traffic fatalities, especially as a complement to underlying road design improvements, and neighborhood residents frequently request them to slow traffic in dangerous areas.

Top 10 Total Fines Collected in September 2018

Full and complete crash data for the city is not available, but using available data from 2008-2017, we find that many of the highest-revenue speed cameras are located in areas with high speed-related crash activity.
